# Glass Bead Game — Content and Audiovisual Reference Set

## Status

Director decisions CAV-001 through CAV-004 are accepted. CAV-005 through CAV-010 remain pending before their named M3/M4/content-production boundaries.

This is a working reference set, not the final content pack and not a source of accepted factual claims. Existing prototype copy is used only to identify promising examples. Every documented relation still requires normalized facets, a reviewed relation type, evidence class, and at least one verified source under the later content task.

## Purpose

The reference set gives design, content, domain, scene, audio, UI, accessibility, and playtest work a shared set of examples. It should answer:

- What makes each intention intellectually distinct?
- What may resonance preview suggest without revealing an authored answer?
- How should intention choice change form, sound, motion, space, and consequence?
- What must remain legible with color, motion, bloom, or sound reduced?
- How can the same pair support more than one honest interpretation?
- Which decisions require the game design director rather than implementation inference?

## Reference principles

1. An intention describes the player's reading, not a hidden authored classification they must guess.
2. A documented relation may confirm, refine, complicate, or cross-cut the declared intention.
3. Resonance preview is relation-neutral and never exposes source text, exact outcome, or a hidden endpoint list.
4. Strong resonance means “rich material for thought,” not “correct answer.”
5. Visual, musical, spatial, and textual channels reinforce one semantic event without becoming identical metaphors.
6. No category depends only on hue, consonance, intensity, or a label.
7. Gesture changes articulation and phrasing, never intellectual validity.
8. Silence, fragility, and unresolved behavior are valid outcomes.

## Cross-category grammar

| Intention | Intellectual verb | Motion/form | Musical behavior | Spatial/systemic consequence | Non-color/accessibility signature |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Echo | correspond, recur, rhyme | mirrored pulses, repeated contour, bilateral or phase-aligned growth | motif imitation, rhythmic alignment, consonant or shared interval structure | the pair answers across space; repeated structure becomes easier to perceive | paired repeating pattern; “shared form” text/caption |
| Passage | carry, influence, translate, transform | directional flow, relay, material changing along the path | one motif transforms into the other; registral/timbral handoff | energy/information visibly travels from source toward destination | arrow/flow texture and ordered pulse; “carries or transforms” text/caption |
| Tension | oppose, contradict, complicate | torsion, counter-motion, suspended separation, unstable phase | controlled beating, dissonance, displaced rhythm, unresolved cadence | the relationship remains active and unsettled rather than failing | alternating/crossed pattern and persistent instability cue; “opposes or complicates” text/caption |
| Ground | support, embody, enable, evidence | anchoring, inward settling, roots/support plane, mass below surface | lower register, pedal, stabilizing rhythm or spectral foundation | one concept gives the other material, logical, or evidential support | weighted/base pattern and low-frequency/text equivalent; “supports or embodies” text/caption |

These are category invariants, not final art/audio recipes. A concept's own identity and the current world must transform the grammar rather than receiving a generic effect pasted onto it.

## Resonance-preview baseline

Before an intention is chosen, candidates may communicate only a neutral resonance band:

- **High:** multiple compatible facets or structures make the pair especially generative, whether documented or open.
- **Medium:** at least one specific interpretable bridge exists, but its direction or intention remains ambiguous.
- **Weak:** little current support is known; commitment may remain fragile, silent, or become a focused Open Thread.

Proposed neutral preview channels:

- density or clarity of a shared pulse, not category-specific rhythm;
- breathing amplitude and label/shape legibility;
- spatial call-and-response without direction, mirroring, tension, or grounding grammar;
- accessible text such as “strong resonance,” never a score or “likely match”;
- no authored title, source, relation type, or endpoint count.

## Reference pair A — Fibonacci Sequence ↔ Counterpoint

### Why it belongs

This is the binding golden-path opening example. The prototype relation “The Spiral Canon” connects proportional growth and displaced recurring voices, with a Bartók/Fibonacci claim that must receive reviewed sourcing during M3.

### Primary reference intention: Echo

- **Interpretation:** recurring proportional structure is heard as a structural rhyme across number and polyphony.
- **Neutral preview:** staggered pulses become mutually legible, but do not reveal Bartók, a fugue, a golden section, or the word Echo.
- **After Echo choice — visual:** two procedural contours repeat with changed scale/phase; the thread develops bilaterally rather than flowing one way.
- **After Echo choice — audio:** the Fibonacci concept motif returns as imitative entries in the Counterpoint timbre; alignment is recognizable without becoming a plain unison.
- **After commit:** a repeated-form cue enters the web; later Canon motif logic may reference the shared facet.
- **Accessible equivalent:** “The two ideas repeat a related structure at different scales or entries.”

### Honest alternate readings

- **Passage:** a numeric proportion becomes compositional organization. This reading is directional and should transform number into voice.
- **Ground:** proportion supplies a formal scaffold for musical construction. This reading should anchor rather than imitate.
- **Tension:** only credible if the player identifies a complication, such as organic-growth metaphor versus deliberate compositional process; it should not be rejected merely because Echo is the authored showcase.

### Open questions

- Does the golden path require the authored relation to accept all four intentions, or may it explicitly refine some as stronger/weaker readings?
- Is the player shown “Bartók” as part of this pair, or must Bartók be a separate concept/revelation identity?
- Which exact source supports the numerical and formal claims?

## Reference pair B — Prime Numbers ↔ Polyrhythm

### Why it belongs

This is the second binding golden-path relation. The prototype “Indivisible Rhythms” describes prime-length rhythmic/chord cycles that delay realignment.

### Primary reference intention: Echo

- **Interpretation:** indivisibility and non-coincident cycles rhyme across arithmetic and rhythm.
- **Neutral preview:** two pulses reveal rich non-repetition without displaying their exact periods or identifying a correct pair.
- **After Echo choice — visual:** separate orbiting markers recur without immediately sharing an origin; a repeating non-alignment pattern becomes visible.
- **After Echo choice — audio:** co-prime pulse cycles interlock and only rarely coincide; neither line becomes accompaniment.
- **After commit:** the pair adds temporal breadth and may contribute to a Canon-like motif if another transformed recurrence joins it.
- **Accessible equivalent:** “Independent cycles share a pattern of resisting simple division and return.”

### Honest alternate readings

- **Ground:** prime structure provides the formal basis for the polyrhythm's long non-repeating cycle.
- **Passage:** arithmetic design is translated into musical time.
- **Tension:** competing cycles pull against periodic closure; the tension need not resolve.

### Open questions

- Should the reference use Messiaen and the specific 17/29 example in the slice, or reserve it for sourced revelation copy?
- Does this pair form part of Canon, Bridge, or only the broader web that enables a motif?

## Reference pair C — Perspective ↔ Renaissance

### Why it belongs

The prototype “The Wall That Opened” treats linear perspective as both technique and a way the Renaissance reorganized pictorial space around a human viewpoint.

### Primary reference intention: Passage

- **Interpretation:** geometry and technique pass into cultural representation, transforming how space and the viewer are staged.
- **Neutral preview:** a planar grid and historical/architectural motif show compatible structure without revealing direction or Masaccio.
- **After Passage choice — visual:** a flat diagram opens directionally into depth; material and line character change as the signal crosses the thread.
- **After Passage choice — audio:** a measured motif transfers into a more spatially layered orchestration, preserving recognizable intervals while changing articulation.
- **After commit:** the world gains a subtle depth/vanishing response tied to this thread rather than a universal unlock.
- **Accessible equivalent:** “A technique is carried into a historical way of seeing.”

### Honest alternate readings

- **Ground:** mathematical perspective materially supports Renaissance spatial representation.
- **Echo:** the era's human-centered worldview and single viewpoint may be interpreted as mirrored structures, but this needs careful wording.
- **Tension:** perspectival order may complicate lived, multiple, or non-geometric perception; this reading should remain an interpretation unless supported by a documented counterpoint.

### Open questions

- Is “Renaissance” too broad for the final 24-concept granularity rule?
- Should the slice replace it with a specific work, technique, workshop, or historical formation?

## Reference pair D — Consonance ↔ Dissonance

### Why it belongs

The prototype “One Continuum” treats consonance and dissonance as historically moving regions of one perceptual continuum rather than moral opposites.

### Primary reference intention: Tension

- **Interpretation:** stability and instability define and complicate one another; neither has meaning in isolation.
- **Neutral preview:** the pair exhibits high mutual energy without resolving toward one endpoint or labeling one as correct.
- **After Tension choice — visual:** the thread twists through alternating phase and spacing; it remains intact and legible rather than flashing as an error.
- **After Tension choice — audio:** controlled beating and suspended intervals establish friction with bounded loudness and voice lifetime; automatic consonant resolution is forbidden.
- **After commit:** the web retains a readable unresolved energy that can later be grounded or reframed by a third relation.
- **Accessible equivalent:** “The ideas oppose and define each other without being forced into agreement.”

### Honest alternate readings

- **Passage:** musical history moves intervals across the cultural boundary between dissonant and consonant.
- **Ground:** acoustical beating/partial structure grounds part of the perceptual distinction.
- **Echo:** both are classifications of interval relationships, but this is weaker and should not flatten their opposition.

### Open questions

- Are these concepts too mutually defined to be separate slice beads, or are they valuable overt Tension teaching material?
- What comfort limits apply to the Tension audio prototype?

## Reference pair E — Abstract Algebra ↔ Energy Conservation

### Why it belongs

The prototype “Noether's Mirror” relates continuous symmetry to conservation laws. It is a strong candidate for Ground because formal structure supplies an explanatory basis for a physical invariant.

### Primary reference intention: Ground

- **Interpretation:** symmetry gives the formal foundation from which conservation behavior follows.
- **Neutral preview:** transformations leave an invariant perceptible without naming Noether or a conservation law.
- **After Ground choice — visual:** one motif settles below the other as a stable transformation lattice; the physical motif continues moving while its invariant base remains.
- **After Ground choice — audio:** a low stable structure persists while surface transformations change rhythm/timbre without losing an invariant relationship.
- **After commit:** the thread contributes a stabilizing structural layer, not points or generic calm.
- **Accessible equivalent:** “A formal symmetry supports and explains a physical conservation.”

### Honest alternate readings

- **Passage:** abstract mathematical symmetry is translated into a physical law.
- **Echo:** invariance under transformation rhymes across formal and physical descriptions.
- **Tension:** credible only when framed as abstraction versus measurement or ideal symmetry versus broken symmetry; the default pair alone does not assert that conflict.

### Open questions

- Is Abstract Algebra sufficiently specific for the slice, or should the bead be Noether's Theorem or Continuous Symmetry?
- Which concept identity best preserves player comprehension without sacrificing precision?

## Open Thread reference — Fibonacci Sequence ↔ Perspective

This pair illustrates an outcome that may be interpretable without an accepted documented relation in the slice.

- **Possible player Echo:** repeated proportion in natural/constructed form and perspectival ordering.
- **Honest response shape:** identify the declared intention and a specific question—for example whether a shared proportional device can be demonstrated in a particular work—without claiming Renaissance artists used Fibonacci design.
- **Visual/audio response:** a fragile partial alignment that does not bloom into the documented-relation hierarchy.
- **Required language:** “You are asking whether…” or equivalent, plus the known shared facet and explicit unresolved status.
- **Forbidden language:** invented influence, generic “everything is connected” praise, or presenting the golden ratio as universal evidence.

## Weak/silent reference — a pair with no credible current support

- Preserve the committed interpretation and gesture if the domain permits the thread.
- Respond with near-silence, translucence, or incomplete growth rather than failure red, point loss, or fabricated prose.
- Offer a precise unresolved observation only if a real facet is available; otherwise say that the Game has no grounded relation yet.
- Keep the result distinguishable from a documented relation and from a richer Open Thread.
